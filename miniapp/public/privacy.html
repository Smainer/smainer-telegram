Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Overview

Smainer is a decentralized AI compute marketplace. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data We Do NOT Collect

Smainer does not collect, store, or share personal data:

  • No personal identifiable information (PII)
  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • No analytics or behavioral data
  • No message content beyond processing requirements

How We Process Data

When you use Smainer via Telegram, your messages are processed in real-time to route AI compute requests to GPU providers. Message content is transmitted to the selected compute provider for inference and is not stored by Smainer.

Wallet Addresses

When you connect a Starknet wallet, your public wallet address is used solely for on-chain transaction processing. Wallet addresses are not linked to any personal identity and are only used to verify payments and settle compute tasks on the blockchain.

Third-Party Services

Smainer interacts with the Starknet blockchain and decentralized GPU providers. These services operate independently and have their own privacy practices. All on-chain transactions are publicly visible on the blockchain by design.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted communications (HTTPS/WSS), signature verification for all callbacks, and no persistent storage of user data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or our practices, contact us at privacy@smainer.io.
